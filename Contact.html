How can we help?

How can I cancel a booking?

If you would like to contact a booking the best thing to do is to reach out to us by phone or by email. Our phone number is 210-123-4567 and email is brookplace@eat.com

Do we have any vegetarian, vegan, gluten free options?

Yes! We understand many have different diets and different beliefs, ask one of our waiters for our Green Menu!

Can we accommodate larger groups? Can we stay open up later for larger groups?

Any group larger than 20 should send us a email at brookplace@eat.com from there we can accomodate the group and leave it open. From there both sides can decided till what time Brooks will stay open.

Are we hiring?

If anyone would like to work at Brooks it is recommended to send your resume to brookplace@eat.com from here someone will look into the request and contact you.
